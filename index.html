About Us


"Navigating complex compliance and regulatory issues with clarity and ease.


As the Anti Money Laundering (AML) and Regulatory landscape continues to become more stringent, it is vital to appoint service providers that have a competent, experienced and diligent team that is committed to ensuring that clients are meeting their AML and regulatory obligations


Based in the Cayman Islands, Canopy Consulting is a specialist AML and Regulatory Advisory and Consulting firm, with a core focus on clients in the financial services industry. The Firm is independent of any other service provider which therefore reduces potential risks associated with real or perceived conflicts of interests.

Pursuant to the Cayman Islands' AML Regime requirements, an AML/CFT audit based on the nature, complexity and risks identified must be conducted on a regular basis. Canopy Consulting can conduct the required audits and review the overall AML programme subject to the AML requirements.


AMLand CTF Training


In compliance with the Cayman Islands' AML Regime's training requirements, Canopy Consulting can offer a solution which is tailored and verifiable. This training can be provided to appropriate staff and senior management that are required to have AML training on an annual basis. The training material is developed specifically to cover the Cayman Islands' Money Laundering and Terrorist and Proliferation Financing Prevention requirements and is updated on a regular basis to ensure that any relevant updates such as sanctions or international advisories are adequately covered.
